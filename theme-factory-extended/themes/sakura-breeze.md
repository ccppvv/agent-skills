# Sakura Breeze

A soft and romantic cherry blossom theme with fresh spring vibes, perfect for elegant presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Sakura Pink**: `#ffb7b2` - Primary color
- **Soft White**: `#fff0f5` - Background color
- **Leaf Green**: `#98fb98` - Accent color
- **Bark Brown**: `#8b4513` - Text color

## Typography

- **Headers**: Noto Serif JP
- **Body Text**: Noto Sans JP

## Best Used For

Wedding planning, beauty and skincare, spring events, women's brands, romantic content.
