# Cyberpunk Neon

A high-contrast neon cyberpunk style with an extremely futuristic feel, perfect for cutting-edge technology presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Neon Pink**: `#ff00ff` - Primary accent color
- **Cyber Blue**: `#00ffff` - Secondary accent color
- **Night Black**: `#0a0a0a` - Deep background
- **Matrix Green**: `#00ff41` - Highlights and data points

## Typography

- **Headers**: Orbitron Bold
- **Body Text**: Roboto Mono

## Best Used For

Tech launches, gaming presentations, future trend reports, AI/ML showcases, digital innovation content.
