# Bauhaus Construct

Classic Bauhaus style with red, yellow, and blue primary colors representing geometric aesthetics.

## Color Palette

- **Bauhaus Red**: `#d12d28` - Main visual focus
- **Construction Blue**: `#2d5d9b` - Secondary geometric blocks
- **Primary Yellow**: `#f6c300` - Highlight elements
- **Canvas White**: `#f0f0f0` - Minimalist background

## Typography

- **Headers**: Futura Bold
- **Body Text**: Helvetica Neue

## Best Used For

Architectural design, art exhibitions, creative education, design theory, modernist content.
