# Electric Indigo

Electric indigo with high energy and mysterious tech trend vibes.

## Color Palette

- **Electric Indigo**: `#6f00ff` - Visual impact primary color
- **Shocking Lime**: `#ccff00` - Extreme contrast color
- **Deep Black**: `#000000` - Background
- **Pure White**: `#ffffff` - Sharp text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat
- **Body Text**: Raleway

## Best Used For

Esports, avant-garde technology, trend magazines, sports brands, high-energy content.
