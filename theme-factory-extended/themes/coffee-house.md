# Coffee House

Rich coffee house tones with warm and textured brown tones.

## Color Palette

- **Espresso Brown**: `#4b3621` - Deep primary tone
- **Latte Beige**: `#f5f5dc` - Soft background
- **Caramel**: `#af6e4d` - Warm accent
- **Foam White**: `#fffdd0` - Text highlight

## Typography

- **Headers**: Abril Fatface
- **Body Text**: Merriweather

## Best Used For

Coffee roasting, bookstores, vintage handcrafts, slow living content, cozy cafes.
