# Desert Sage

Desert sage with low-saturation earth tones representing natural wilderness.

## Color Palette

- **Sage Green**: `#9dc183` - Main natural color
- **Sandstone**: `#c2b280` - Background base
- **Terracotta Red**: `#e2725b` - Warm accent
- **Cactus Dark**: `#2f4f4f` - Text color

## Typography

- **Headers**: Bitter
- **Body Text**: Source Sans Pro

## Best Used For

Outdoor adventure, organic cotton, nature documentaries, camping brands, desert landscape content.
