# Berry Smoothie

Berry smoothie colors with sweet and refreshing purple tones.

## Color Palette

- **Berry Purple**: `#800080` - Rich primary color
- **Yogurt Pink**: `#ffb6c1` - Soft background
- **Blueberry Dark**: `#483d8b` - Text/dark blocks
- **Fresh Mint**: `#98ff98` - Refreshing accent

## Typography

- **Headers**: Pacifico
- **Body Text**: Dosis

## Best Used For

Healthy light meals, women's fitness, juice brands, beauty bloggers, wellness content.
