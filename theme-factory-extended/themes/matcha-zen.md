# Matcha Zen

Matcha green with Japanese-style beige tones creating a serene Zen atmosphere.

## Color Palette

- **Matcha Green**: `#88b04b` - Core primary color
- **Rice Paper**: `#f5f5f5` - Background color
- **Dark Tea**: `#4a5d23` - Deep accent/text
- **Bamboo Yellow**: `#e0d68a` - Soft accent

## Typography

- **Headers**: Zen Maru Gothic
- **Body Text**: Muli

## Best Used For

Tea brands, yoga and meditation, health food, cultural exhibitions, wellness content.
