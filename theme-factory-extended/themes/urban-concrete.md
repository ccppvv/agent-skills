# Urban Concrete

Industrial concrete gray with safety yellow creating a modern rough aesthetic.

## Color Palette

- **Concrete Grey**: `#808080` - Main material color
- **Safety Yellow**: `#ffd700` - Eye-catching accent
- **Steel Dark**: `#2f4f4f` - Structural lines
- **Chalk White**: `#f5f5f5` - Text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald
- **Body Text**: Roboto Condensed

## Best Used For

Street fashion brands, construction, extreme sports, modern art, urban culture content.
