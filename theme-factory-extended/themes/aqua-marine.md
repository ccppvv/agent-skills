# Aqua Marine

Clear aquamarine blue representing a transparent underwater world.

## Color Palette

- **Aquamarine**: `#7fffd4` - Clear primary color
- **Deep Ocean**: `#00ced1` - Deep color layers
- **Coral Reef**: `#ff7f50` - Vibrant contrast
- **Pearl White**: `#f0ffff` - Background and shine

## Typography

- **Headers**: Josefin Sans
- **Body Text**: Nunito

## Best Used For

Water sports, seafood dining, resorts, eco-friendly water treatment, ocean conservation content.
