# Deep Space

A profound universe theme combining dark purples with starlight for mysterious presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Void Purple**: `#1a0b2e` - Main deep space background
- **Nebula Pink**: `#764ba2` - Gradient and highlights
- **Starlight**: `#ffffff` - Text and star points
- **Cosmic Blue**: `#667eea` - Secondary glow

## Typography

- **Headers**: Exo 2
- **Body Text**: Open Sans

## Best Used For

Astronomy education, sci-fi novels, immersive experiences, high-end technology, space exploration content.
