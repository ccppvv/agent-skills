# Graphite & Gold

Black-gold business style with understated luxury and professional texture.

## Color Palette

- **Graphite Grey**: `#2a2a2a` - Premium gray background
- **Champagne Gold**: `#d4af37` - Refined embellishment
- **Matte Black**: `#1c1c1c` - Deep layer blocks
- **Off White**: `#f8f8f8` - Clear reading

## Typography

- **Headers**: Didot
- **Body Text**: Garamond

## Best Used For

Financial reports, law firms, high-end consulting, architectural design, luxury business content.
