# Vintage Vinyl

70s retro style with warm brown-orange tones creating a nostalgic feel.

## Color Palette

- **Retro Orange**: `#e76f51` - Vibrant accent color
- **Mustard**: `#e9c46a` - Secondary color
- **Espresso**: `#264653` - Dark background/text
- **Cream Paper**: `#f4f1de` - Light background

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cooper Black
- **Body Text**: Courier New

## Best Used For

Music albums, retro fashion, coffee shops, nostalgic marketing, vintage brands.
