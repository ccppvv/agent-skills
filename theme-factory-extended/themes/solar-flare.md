# Solar Flare

Solar flare with extremely intense red-yellow gradients.

## Color Palette

- **Solar Red**: `#ff4500` - Intense primary color
- **Flare Orange**: `#ffa500` - Transition accent
- **Core Yellow**: `#ffff00` - Center highlight
- **Space Dark**: `#191970` - Strong contrast dark section

## Typography

- **Headers**: Anton
- **Body Text**: Fira Sans

## Best Used For

Promotional activities, sports energy, warning information, rock music, high-energy content.
