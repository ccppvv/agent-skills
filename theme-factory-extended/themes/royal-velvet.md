# Royal Velvet

Royal velvet texture with gold-purple color scheme representing luxury and nobility.

## Color Palette

- **Royal Purple**: `#4b0082` - Main noble color
- **Antique Gold**: `#ffd700` - Luxurious accent
- **Rich Black**: `#121212` - Deep background
- **Silk White**: `#f8f8ff` - Text color

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cinzel
- **Body Text**: Lora

## Best Used For

Jewelry exhibitions, high-end galas, award ceremonies, luxury brand marketing, premium events.
