# Candy Pop

Candy color pop art style with bright and lively happy dopamine vibes.

## Color Palette

- **Bubblegum Pink**: `#ff69b4` - Vibrant primary color
- **Lemon Drop**: `#fff44f` - Bright highlight
- **Sky Blue**: `#87ceeb` - Fresh background
- **Grape Purple**: `#9370db` - Fun accent

## Typography

- **Headers**: Fredoka One
- **Body Text**: Quicksand

## Best Used For

Children's products, dessert shops, holiday celebrations, fun applications, playful brands.
