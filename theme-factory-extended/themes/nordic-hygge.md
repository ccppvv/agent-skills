# Nordic Hygge

Nordic minimalist cozy style with low-saturation warm grays and wood tones.

## Color Palette

- **Warm Grey**: `#cdc7be` - Main background
- **Muted Wood**: `#8d7966` - Accent color
- **Soft Charcoal**: `#4a4a4a` - Text color
- **Wool White**: `#fdfbf7` - Secondary background

## Typography

- **Headers**: Playfair Display
- **Body Text**: Lato

## Best Used For

Home design, lifestyle, psychological counseling, comfort products, wellness content.
