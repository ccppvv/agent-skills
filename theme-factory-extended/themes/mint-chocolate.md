# Mint Chocolate

Mint chocolate combining refreshing coolness with rich chocolate in a classic pairing.

## Color Palette

- **Fresh Mint**: `#3eb489` - Refreshing primary color
- **Dark Chocolate**: `#3c2f2f` - Deep background/text
- **Cream Filling**: `#f5f5f0` - Light areas
- **Cocoa Dust**: `#d2691e` - Warm details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Ubuntu
- **Body Text**: PT Sans

## Best Used For

Dessert baking, fresh home decor, retro fashion, creative stationery, sweet treats.
